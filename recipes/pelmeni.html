Description:

Pelmeni are traditional Russian dumplings made from thin dough filled with seasoned ground meat, usually a mix of pork and beef. Theyâ€™re small, bite-sized pockets that are boiled until tender and often served hot with sour cream, butter, or vinegar. Pelmeni are loved for their simple, comforting taste and are a staple in Russian cuisine, perfect as a hearty meal or snack.

Ingredients:

  • Ground meat (pork, beef, or mix)
  • Flour
  • Egg
  • Water
  • Garlic
  • Sour cream (for serving)
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Make dough with flour, egg, water, and salt.
  2. Mix meat, onion, and seasonings for filling.
  3. Roll dough, cut circles, fill, and seal dumplings.
  4. Boil until they float.
  5. Serve with sour cream or butter.
