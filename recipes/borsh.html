Borsh Soup Recipe

Home

Description:

Borscht is a traditional Eastern European soup made with beets, cabbage, carrots, potatoes, and onions simmered in vegetable or meat broth. A touch of tomato paste, vinegar, or lemon juice balances the beets’ sweetness, while dill and bay leaves add aroma. Served hot or cold, it’s typically finished with sour cream and fresh dill for a creamy, refreshing bite.

Ingredients:

  • Beets
  • Cabbage
  • Carrots
  • Potatoes
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Toamto paste
  • Fresh Dill
  • Salt & pepper

Steps:

  1. Peel and chop/grate vegetables.
  2. Sauté onion, carrots, and beets.
  3. Add broth, potatoes, and cabbage.
  4. Add broth, potatoes, and cabbage.
  5. Simmer until vegetables are tender.
  6. Serve with sour cream and dill.
